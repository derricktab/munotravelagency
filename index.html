WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE

Muno Travel Agency is a fully registered and licensed recruitment company by the Ministry of Gender, Labour and Social development to carry on business of recruitment of Ugandan migrant workers abroad.

As a reputable leader in the employment industry, we provide employment and staffing solutions to clients companies and organizations. Our innovative spirit, community focus and time honored integrity sets us apart as a full-service staffing agency.

Our specialized offerings include direct hire for professional, skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled labour in countries like Turkey, Qatar, United Arab Emirates, Canada, Jordan, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, and many others
